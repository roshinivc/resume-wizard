Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Short version: We do not store your resume or job description after your analysis is complete. Your data is processed in real time and discarded. We do not sell your data to anyone.

1. Who We Are

Resume Wizard ("we," "us," or "our") is an AI-powered resume analysis tool operated by Roshini Velamuri as a sole proprietorship. You can contact us at: velamuri.roshini@gmail.com

2. What Information We Collect

Resume and Job Description Content: When you use Resume Wizard, you upload a resume file (PDF, DOCX, or TXT) and paste a job description. This content is:

  • Transmitted securely to our server
  • Passed to the Anthropic Claude AI API for analysis
  • Returned to you as analysis results
  • Not stored, saved, or logged after your session ends

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics (number of analyses, error rates) to improve the service. This data does not include your resume content or personal information.

Payment Information: If you purchase a subscription or pay-as-you-go analysis, payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card information. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data: stripe.com/privacy

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the resume analysis service
  • To process payments via Stripe
  • To improve the accuracy and quality of our AI analysis
  • To communicate with you about your account (if applicable)

4. Data Retention

Your resume and job description content is processed in real time and is not retained after your analysis is delivered. We do not build a database of user resumes.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): Processes your resume and job description to generate analysis. Subject to Anthropic's privacy policy.
  • Vercel: Hosts our application. Subject to Vercel's privacy policy.
  • Stripe: Processes payments. Subject to Stripe's privacy policy.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not store your resume data, there is nothing to delete or export. If you have an account, you may request deletion by contacting us at velamuri.roshini@gmail.com.

7. Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for authentication and session management only. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

Resume Wizard is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions: velamuri.roshini@gmail.com
